Serve Ads

VendoServe uses reliable and high-availability ad servers that can serve 8000+ ads per second per deployment. VendoServe ad servers are scalable and offers millisecond ad response times imposing minimal latency.

  • Cloud-based ad server or On-premise, dedicated ad server
  • Targeting parameters include:
    • user demographics
    • keywords
    • ad types
  • Log and track events on ads including impressions, clicks and call-to-actions.

Monitor Ad Campaigns

With powerful and centralized management and reporting tools VendoServe gives deep insight into user engagement. VendoServe utilizes Big Data analysis to produce valuable reports and statistics.

  • Real-time campaign metrics
  • Gain insights in terms of:
    • reach
    • demographics
    • location
    • engagement rates
  • A complete reporting API to integrate campaign metrics into custom dashboards
